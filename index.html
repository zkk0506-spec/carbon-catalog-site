Buyer FAQ

Common Questions for Carbon Fiber Buyers

Can you customize carbon fiber tubes, sheets and rods?

Yes. Custom size, thickness, diameter, wall thickness, length, surface finish, fiber pattern and machining service can be quoted based on drawings, samples or application requirements.

What files should buyers send for CNC cut carbon fiber parts?

DXF, STEP, PDF drawings, samples, dimensions, tolerance requirement, material thickness and quantity are useful for fast engineering review.

Do you support small batch trial orders and bulk production?

Yes. The catalog is built around RFQ communication, so buyers can request samples, small batches, mixed specifications or bulk production.

Which documents are available?

The website package includes RoHS certificates/reports and MSDS report files for T300 carbon fiber sheet, tube and fabric.
