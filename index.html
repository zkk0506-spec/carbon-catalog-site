Technical Capabilities

Custom Tube Engineering

Our tube manufacturing process is optimized for diverse engineering needs. We don't just supply tubes; we provide the exact structural component for your project.

  • 1

    Roll-Wrapped Process

    Multiple layers of prepreg wrapped around a mandrel for superior torsional strength and impact resistance.

  • 2

    Pultrusion Technology

    Continuous pultrusion for high-volume, cost-effective solid rods and constant-section profiles.

  • 3

    Custom Surface Finish

    Available in 3K Twill, Plain, UD, Glossy, Matte, and Sanded finishes to meet aesthetic and friction requirements.
